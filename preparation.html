What to Bring to Minnehack

Personal Effects

  • One form of ID (any government-issued or school-issued ID with your birth date and picture on it will be sufficient).

Forms

  • If you are under the age of 18, bring a filled out waiver signed by your parents

Hygiene/Bathroom Supplies

  • In short, deodorant is not optional. Please do not let hygiene become an issue.
  • Toothbrush + toothpaste
  • Hairbrush, if necessary
  • Glasses/Contact Lenses/Saline Solution/Eye drops

Sleeping Items

The sleeping area provided is on tiled floor-not especially comfortable! Please bring whatever necessary to make your sleeping accommodations suitable for yourself.

  • Sleeping bag
  • Soft Mat
  • Pillows
  • Blankets
  • Stuffed animals, if desired

Food

  • Meals and snacks will be provided, with vegetarian options available at all meals.
  • Vegans and people with other strong dietary restrictions should pack meals

Misc

  • Bring your game face
