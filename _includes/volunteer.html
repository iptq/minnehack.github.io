Volunteer!

We're currently looking for volunteers to help with the setup and and administration of MinneHack.

If you're interested in doing any of the following, please sign up and help us make a great event even better!

  • Event setup — preparing the hacking areas, moving furniture, etc.
  • Food prep for breakfast, lunch, and dinner
  • Event teardown — doing the setup stuff, but in reverse

Plus, any volunteers will get a special MinneHack Volunteer T-shirt!

If there's some other way you think you can contribute, shoot us an email, or contact us with the contact form below!
